Entries marked ✓ were verified live during compilation; entries marked ~ are existence-verified but their current version, status or governance is unconfirmed and may already be out of date or contain errors. Spotting and fixing drift in this table is itself a contribution to the group's mission.

Access — Open · web: open, royalty-free, anyone may participate (W3C Community/Working Groups, IETF). Gated: participation or specification controlled by a foundation, single vendor, or paid membership. Columns: Group · Focus · Core elements · Access · Latest/verified · KR / AIAO.
